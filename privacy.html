Sun Valley Simulations — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sun Valley Simulations ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and stores information when you use our Roblox account verification system on our Discord server.

1. What We Collect

When you verify your Roblox account through our system, we collect and store:

  • Your Roblox User ID and Roblox username
  • Your Discord User ID

We do not collect your email address, password, payment information, or any other personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used solely for the following purposes within our Discord server:

  • Linking your Roblox identity to your Discord account
  • Automatically updating your Discord nickname to reflect your in-game team and callsign
  • Assigning Discord roles based on your in-game status

Your data is never used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose outside of Sun Valley Simulations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your Roblox and Discord information stays within our server systems only.

4. Data Storage

Your verified account data is stored locally on our server in a private database file. It is not uploaded to any public cloud service or accessible externally.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you are a member of our Discord server or until you request deletion. We do not automatically remove data when you leave the server.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request to view the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data at any time

To make a request, contact server administration directly through the Sun Valley Simulations Discord server.

7. Third-Party Services

Our verification system uses the Roblox OAuth 2.0 API to confirm your Roblox identity. By authorizing our app, you also agree to Roblox's Terms of Use and their data practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of our verification system after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any questions or data requests, please reach out to server staff in the Sun Valley Simulations Discord server.

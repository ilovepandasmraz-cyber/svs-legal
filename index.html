Roblox Account Verification

Link Your Roblox Account

Sun Valley Simulations uses Roblox OAuth to securely verify your Roblox identity within our Discord server. Verification allows us to automatically sync your in-game team, callsign, and nickname.

How it works

1
Run /verify in our Discord server
2
Click the link and authorize with your Roblox account
3
Your Roblox and Discord accounts are now linked — you're done

We only request your Roblox User ID and username. We do not access your email, password, or any other account information.
